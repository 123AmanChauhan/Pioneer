OFFICE SECURITY

Security Service for Corporates in India

Role of a Security Personnel working in office has undergone drastic change. Not a long ago, security guard in office was primarily deployed to manned the gate. But over the last decade or so, there is a complete changeover of role of security in a corporate office in Mumbai.

In Today’s time, security team is expected to follow the SOP set up by the client and carry out number of important task for the safety and security of office premises.

Our Security broadly performs following functions at corporate offices:

  • Access Control
  • Managing Reception Desk
  • Receiving Guest
  • Gate Pass Management
  • Key Management
  • Maintenance of registers, records and files
  • Safe custody of items lost and found
  • Frisking housekeeping and other labourers
  • Offer safety warning and tips at the time of crisis
  • Coordination with external agencies
  • Reporting to Admin/Security head

Besides above functions, our security is capable of following additional special tasks:

  • Fire Fighting
  • First Aid
  • CCTV Operation
  • Basic Disaster Management

We design the security solution, taking into account client’s business needs. We provide job specific onsite training to the guard at the time of deployment and repeat it every 3 months or as per the site requirement to increase effectiveness and productivity of our guards. Our tailor made solution makes us top corporate security company in Mumbai.

Want to experience professional corporate security, but having budget constraint? Get in touch with us and experience professional corporate security solution within your budget.
