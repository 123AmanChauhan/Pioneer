OFFICE BOY

Office Boy act as a helping hand to your office staffs by carrying out daily small routine jobs which otherwise is handled by the employees. Our office boy service, also known as Support Staff Service, in Mumbai helps you in saving precious time of your office staff and enable them to perform their task with higher productivity and energy.

Daily routine task carried out by Globe’s office boy are listed below:

  • Handling inward and outward movement of documents.
  • Carrying out print, copy, scan and fax work.
  • Messenger service by attending outdoor work like visiting banks, clients, vendors, post office, etc.
  • Filing and retrieving of documents.
  • Maintenance of records, registers and books in orderly manner.
  • Administrating movement of documents and keeping record of the same.
  • Managing courier and other documents movement inside and outside the office.
  • Provide support to employees and assist them in performing their official duties.

Advantages of hiring office boy from Globe:

  1. Reliever is provided in case office boy is absent.
  2. Immediate replacement in case of non-performance.
  3. Trained and experience office boy who knows his job well.
  4. Apart from regular training, on the job training is provided to make him understand exact nature of work and what is expected of him.
  5. Uniformed and professional look.
  6. On time payment of salary directly in their account.
  7. Total statutory compliance.
