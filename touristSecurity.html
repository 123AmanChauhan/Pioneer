TOURIST SECURITY

Tourist Protection Service in India for Foreigners and Expats

India offers an excellent travel experience which is second to none and arguably cannot be witnessed anywhere else in the world. India is a country with rich heritage and diverse culture wherein every few hundred kilometers language and culture changes. Right from North to South to East to West there is something store for everyone.

But with growing security concerns in recent times, visitors are afraid of travelling to India. In such a scenario, we step in to secure your travel and keep you safe all the time, no matter where you are travelling in India. Our Personal Protection Officer in India will be with you 24/7 to protect and guide you in your journey. To make you feel safer, we can offer a 24/7 real time tracking solution where a small portable GPS tracking device shall be with you and your movement can be monitored by us for security purpose.

Our head office is located in Mumbai, which is the safest city in India. But you may require a close protection officer, if you are visiting outside the city to places such as Goa, Delhi, Taj Mahal Agra, Kerala, etc. You may like to hire Personal Protection Officer in Mumbai, if you are visiting populated areas and slums to keep notorious people and street vendors at bay.

PSO Cum Driver Service

Personal Security Officer (PSO) can work as a driver to enable you to save cost on hiring two individuals and also occupying space in a vehicle. Our PSO cum Driver service in Mumbai comes with a valid driving license. PSO cum Driver is physically fit and mentally alert person with local knowledge. He is a skilled driver to take you wherever you desire. You can hire PSO cum Driver Service in Mumbai for a trip, short term basis or long term basis. For hiring PSO cum Driver on short term basis, you need to inform us at least a day in advance to enable us make the required arrangement.
